About the Alumni Association

The DBIT Alumni Association connects graduates across engineering, management, research, entrepreneurship, defence, public service and global careers. It exists to strengthen alumni bonds, support current students, celebrate achievements and bring industry exposure back to the campus.

The association reflects the DBIT ecosystem across CSE, CSE-AI&ML, CSE-AI&DS, CSE-IoT&CS, ISE, ECE, EEE, MBA and PhD programs. Alumni engagement includes mentorship, guest talks, reunions, placement support, technical events and recognition of outstanding graduates.

Mission

To build a supportive, active and professionally valuable alumni network that mentors students, enables career opportunities, encourages entrepreneurship and keeps every DBIT graduate connected to the institution.

Vision

To make DBIT Alumni Association a trusted lifelong platform where alumni collaborate across countries, companies and disciplines while contributing to DBIT's growth as an autonomous, industry-connected institution.
